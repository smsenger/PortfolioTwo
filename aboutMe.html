From one creative profession to another

After earning a PhD in Musicology and Ethnomusicology, I chose to pursue a career in software development to put my work ethic, resourcefulness, and attention to detail to good use. In my work as a full stack developer in the Atlanta metropolitan area, I bring dedication and commitment to my endeavors. My experience with Python, JavaScript, Node.js, React, HTML, CSS, and GitHub, among other technologies, allows me to utilize broad resources to successfully complete projects. With interests in both front-end and back-end development and the ability to work independently or with a team, I welcome the challenge to make any product all it can be.
